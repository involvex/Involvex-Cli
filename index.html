Privacy Policy

InvolveX CLI does not collect personal data, analytics, or telemetry. All configuration is stored locally at %USERPROFILE%\.involvex-cli and never transmitted off-device by default.

Optional integrations (such as Discord RPC) communicate directly with the respective third-party APIs using credentials you provide. Disabling those integrations stops all outbound communication.

Log files generated by the CLI remain on your machine inside the logs/ directory. You may delete them at any time.
